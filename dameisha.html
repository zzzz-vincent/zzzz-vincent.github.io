CO-MILIEU is a design collective established by myself and my friends. We have regular work across the work, and are adaptable and undertake design projects as per our interests and availability.
Designer: qinrong, qiaoqi, and me
 
In the middle of 2021, a major Chinese real estate developer contacted us regarding a concept design for the area surrounding their headquarters. This area, called Dameisha, is located in another Bay Area (Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area) and is known for its proximity to scenic beaches and mountains, just a 30-minute drive from the city center of Shenzhen.
Our design incorporated a new working system that seamlessly integrates into the existing urban environment and the surrounding mountain-ocean landscape. We strategically placed new structures along walkways for various purposes such as cafes, art exhibitions, and outdoor performances, to enhance the overall experience of the area.
